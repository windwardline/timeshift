# Light Boxes and Light Devices

When artificial bright light can stand in for sunshine.

## Why use a device

Natural daylight is the best source, but it is not always available at the hour you need it —
a dark winter morning, a windowless hotel, or a layover indoors. A light box or similar bright
device can deliver a strong dose of light on demand to help shift the clock.

## Timing still rules

A light device follows the same timing logic as sunlight: use it in the morning to advance
the clock for eastward travel, and in the evening to delay it for westward travel. The wrong
timing pushes the clock the wrong way just as mistimed sunlight does.

## Sensible use

Bright light is generally safe for short, timed sessions, but people with certain eye or
mood conditions should check with a clinician first. Treat a device as a backup for when
daylight is not available rather than a replacement for getting outdoors.
