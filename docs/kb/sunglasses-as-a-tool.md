# Sunglasses as a Timing Tool

A simple way to withhold light exactly when it would push you the wrong way.

## Blocking unwanted light

Sunglasses let you dim bright light during the window when it would shift your clock in the
unwanted direction. They turn light avoidance into something portable you can use in an
airport, on arrival, or during a layover.

## When to wear them

On an eastward trip, sunglasses help during very early morning light you want to avoid until
true local morning. On a westward trip, they help blunt early-morning light so you can keep
delaying your clock. Take them off when you actually want the light's signal.

## A supporting role

Sunglasses do not move the clock on their own — they only prevent mistimed light from
interfering. Pair them with deliberately seeking bright light at the right hour for your
direction, and they make that plan cleaner.
