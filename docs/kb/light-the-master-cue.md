# Light, the Master Cue

Why light is the single most powerful tool for moving your clock.

## The strongest signal

Of all the cues that set the body clock, light is by far the most powerful. Bright light in
the morning tends to shift the clock earlier, while bright light in the evening shifts it
later. Because light dominates, getting its timing right matters more than any supplement.

## Timing decides direction

The same bright light advances or delays the clock depending on when it lands relative to
your body's night. Light after your temperature minimum advances the clock; light before it
delays the clock. Mistimed light can actively push you the wrong way.

## Outdoors beats indoors

Even an overcast sky outdoors is many times brighter than typical indoor lighting and gives
the clock a far stronger signal. When you want light, getting outside is the most effective
move; when you want to avoid it, indoors and sunglasses help.
