# Pre-Flight Meal Timing

Using mealtimes to reinforce a pre-trip clock shift.

## Meals as a secondary cue

Meal timing is a clock cue that backs up light and sleep timing. As you shift your sleep
schedule in the days before a trip, nudging breakfast, lunch, and dinner in the same
direction strengthens the overall signal to your body.

## Match the direction

Going east, move meals earlier along with your earlier sleep; going west, move them later.
Arriving with your hunger rhythm already closer to local time eases the digestive side of
jetlag, which is driven by the gut's own clock.

## Keep it simple

You do not need a strict diet — just consistent meal times that march in the right direction.
Combined with pre-trip light and sleep shifts, adjusted mealtimes help you step off the plane
already leaning toward destination time.
