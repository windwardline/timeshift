# Short Trips: Don't Fully Shift

Why a quick trip can be easier if you stay partly on home time.

## Adapting may not be worth it

For a trip of only a day or two, fully adapting to the destination and then flipping straight
back can be more disruptive than not shifting at all. Each adjustment takes days, so a short
stay may end before you finish — and then you have to undo it.

## Anchor to home time

A common tactic is to keep your home schedule as much as the trip allows, and to schedule
important activities for hours when you would naturally be alert at home. This minimizes the
clock disruption you will have to reverse on return.

## Mind the essentials

Even when not shifting, protect sleep where you can, stay hydrated, and use short strategic
naps to stay functional. The goal is to limit damage rather than chase a full adaptation you
will only have to undo.
