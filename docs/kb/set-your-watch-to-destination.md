# Set Your Watch to Destination Time

A small habit that frames every in-flight decision.

## The mental switch

Changing your watch — and your thinking — to destination time as you board is a simple but
powerful first move. Once "now" means the time at your destination, every choice about
sleeping, eating, and seeking light has an obvious answer.

## It guides your choices

If it is destination night, that is when to try to sleep; if it is destination day, that is
when to stay awake and seek light. Without the switch, travelers tend to drift along on home
time and arrive having done nothing to shift their clock.

## Reinforce on arrival

Keep living on the destination clock the moment you land — meals, sleep, and activity all on
local time. The watch change is the start of adopting local time, which is the core of fast
adjustment.
