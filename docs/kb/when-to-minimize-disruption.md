# When to Just Minimize Disruption

Choosing to limit damage instead of chasing full adjustment.

## A legitimate strategy

Sometimes the right call is not to adapt at all but simply to reduce the toll: stay hydrated,
nap strategically, protect sleep where you can, and accept being a bit off. For very short
trips and back-to-back travel, this beats an adjustment you cannot finish.

## When it fits

Minimizing disruption fits short stays, rapid round-trips, and situations where full
adaptation is not practical or would have to be undone immediately. The aim is to stay
functional, not to fully reset your clock.

## How to do it well

Keep important activities in your naturally alert hours, use short naps and caffeine
judiciously, guard your sleep, and skip alcohol. Choosing to minimize disruption is a planned
strategy, not a failure to adjust.
