# In-Flight Strategy

What to do in the air to arrive closer to destination time.

## Think in destination time

Set your watch and your mindset to destination time as you board. Deciding it is now "night in
Tokyo" or "morning in London" frames every in-flight choice about when to sleep, eat, and seek
light, so you start living on the new schedule before you land.

## Sleep on destination night

Once you are on destination time, try to sleep during the hours that are night at your
destination and stay awake when it is daytime there. On an eastward red-eye that means
sleeping for the destination's night even if it is still evening back home.

## Manage the basics

Use an eye mask and dim your screen during your target sleep window, move around periodically,
keep drinking water, and eat in line with destination mealtimes. These small choices reduce
both jetlag and ordinary travel fatigue.
